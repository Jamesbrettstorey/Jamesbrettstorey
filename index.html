Core Concept

The Mask Changes Everything

Spirit Masks is an open-world fantasy RPG built around a single, radical mechanic: the masks you wear define who you are. Each mask you collect reshapes your persona, transforms your stats, unlocks new abilities — and exposes new vulnerabilities. The key to survival is knowing when to switch.

You begin as a humble villager in a world at peace, setting out from a small central village with nothing but a basic wooden mask. What follows is a journey through elemental realms, ancient mysteries, and a growing threat that only someone willing to embrace many faces can confront.

"Masks are not disguises here. They are identity. The rare ones — the legendary masks — they don't just change your face. They change what you are capable of." — From the Concept Notes
⚔️

Strategic Combat

Every enemy has elemental strengths and weaknesses. Swap masks mid-battle to exploit openings and survive encounters that would otherwise be impossible.

🌍

Open World Exploration

A fog-of-war map gradually revealed as you explore. Multiple elemental realms — Earth, Fire, Water, Wind — each with their own terrain, enemies, and secrets.

📈

Dual Progression

Level up to gain small universal stat boosts. But the real power comes from the masks — each one delivers dramatic, specialised shifts that no amount of levelling can match.

🧩

Non-Linear Choice

After the starting zone, you choose which elemental realm to enter. No forced path. Your mask collection shapes both your options and your playstyle.

The World

A Fantasy Realm of Elemental Kingdoms

The world of Spirit Masks is a classic high-fantasy setting — no modern technology, no machines. Forests thick with ancient magic, valleys carved by forgotten wars, volcanoes that breathe fire and purpose, oceans that hide civilisations beneath their surface.

At the centre of it all is a small, quiet village — where your story begins. The world is at peace when you arrive. That peace will not last.

Each elemental realm is distinct not just in appearance but in culture, architecture, enemies, and the masks they guard. The masks of each realm reflect the nature of the land itself.

🏔 Earth Realm

Mountain villages, stone fortresses, ancient quarries. Home of physical warriors and the Earth Mask.

🌋 Fire Realm

Volcanic plains, ash cities, forges that never cool. Aggressive enemies who hit first and ask later.

🌊 Water Realm

Coastal cities, flooded ruins, underwater passages. Enemies who heal and adapt to your tactics.

🌪 Wind Realm

Sky bridges, cliff-side settlements, tempests. The fastest and most evasive enemies in the game.

Progression

Your Journey Through the Realms

The world is structured around a level-based progression that opens new zones and masks as you grow. But within each tier, the order is yours to choose.

LEVELS 1 – 10

The Starting Village

Begin with a basic wooden mask. Learn the fundamentals — combat, exploration, mask-swapping. Help the village, learn the lore of masks, and prepare to venture into the realms.

LEVELS 10 – 25 · YOUR CHOICE

The Four Elemental Realms

Earth, Fire, Water, Wind. Enter in any order. Each realm has its own central city hub, quests, bosses, and the elemental mask of that realm waiting to be earned.

LEVELS 25 – 40 · ADVANCED

The Advanced Elemental Zones

Lightning, Ice, Poison. Harder enemies, more complex mask interactions, and masks that require experience to wield effectively. The world's deeper threats begin to stir.

LEVELS 40+ · ENDGAME

The Final Zone

The main villain's domain. Everything you have collected — every mask, every ability, every tradeoff you've mastered — will be needed here. The spirit that wears all faces must become something more.

"Experience is earned through blood and service — cutting down enemies or aiding the villages that shelter you. Whether simply walking the world grants wisdom is a question the elders still debate." — On the Nature of Growth

World Lore

The Culture of the Mask

In this world, masks are not unusual. Everyone wears one. A basic mask is a birthright — modest, functional, a symbol of belonging to the community. But the legendary masks, the ones worn by heroes and feared by enemies, are something else entirely.

An alternate vision of the protagonist has emerged from the design notes: rather than a person wearing a mask, the protagonist may be a spirit — a wisp, invisible to the naked eye. The floating mask is all that others see. It is their body, their face, and their power. This framing makes the mask not a tool the protagonist uses, but the very form they take.

How Masks Are Obtained

  • Defeating realm bosses
  • Solving ancient puzzles
  • Exploration & discovery
  • Completing village quests
  • Co-operative assistance

Mask Tiers

  • Basic masks — worn by all

The Protagonist's Path

  • Collects masks — never discards
  • Swaps strategically, not permanently
