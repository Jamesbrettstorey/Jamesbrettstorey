## 🎭 Spirit Masks — Game Concept

One of my current projects is **Spirit Masks**, an open-world fantasy RPG concept built around a single core mechanic: the masks you wear define who you are.

The concept is documented in [index.html](index.html) — open it in a browser to explore the full interactive page.

### What's on the page

| Section | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Hero** | Title, tagline, and introduction to the game's premise |
| **Concept** | The four pillars — strategic combat, open world exploration, dual progression, and non-linear choice |
| **The Masks** | All 8 elemental mask types, a detailed Earth Mask stat breakdown, and the counter-relationship chart between mask types |
| **The World** | The four elemental realms (Earth, Fire, Water, Wind) and what makes each one distinct |
| **Progression** | The level-zone structure — from the starting village (1–10) through the elemental realms (10–25), advanced zones (25–40), to the final endgame area |
| **Lore** | The world's mask culture, how masks are obtained, mask tiers, the spirit/wisp protagonist concept, and open design questions |

### Core idea in brief

- Collect and swap **masks** that dramatically shift your stats, abilities, and elemental resistances
- Each mask has strong advantages paired with clear weaknesses — no perfect mask, only the right one for the moment
- Eight elements: Earth, Fire, Water, Wind, Lightning, Ice, Life, Death — with rock-paper-scissors style counters
- Non-linear open world split into elemental realms, each with its own hub city, quests, and boss mask reward
- Protagonist starts as a simple villager with a basic wooden mask and grows into something far greater
